So, who is Melodies?

I am an aspiring software engineer/applied mathematician/web developer who loves solving challenging problems and converting novel ideas into reality. Being a double degree (in Math and CS) student, I also believe in the importance of multidiscipline studies in solving real world problems. Since highschool, I have done multiple research projects of a wide range: Biology, Mathematics, Computer Science. I eventually realised that many biological problems like the binding specificity of double-stranded RNA (studied in an A*STAR internship) or growth of cancer (research done during a MIT summer camp) can be modelled using Mathematics and CS, allowing one to gain deeper insights into the mechanism of these biological agents (as it can be impractical or impossible to do so in the lab).

Besides academics, I enjoy challenging myself in the physical aspects and enjoy nature. For example, I enjoy nature hiking and snorkelling in the wild. A picture on the right shows me and my cousins hiking up the "Difficult" trail of Bukit Timah. I also enjoy keeping myself fit and mentally prepared for the next challenge by doing sports! Skateboarding and badminton are some of my favourite sports.
